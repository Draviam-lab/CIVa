CIVa (Chromosomal Instability aiding Variants) database curates Loss of Function (LoF) variation predictions in chromosome segregation genes from multiple human genome sequencing databases, including ELGH (East London Genes and Health), COSMIC (Catalogue of Somatic Mutations in Cancers) and Gnomad (Genome Aggregation Database).

The first version was published as part of Asifa et al., 2021, DOI to be included.
