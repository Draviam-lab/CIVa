CIVa Database

CIVa (Chromosomal Instability aiding genetic Variants) curates kinetochore gene variation from multiple human genome sequencing databases including ELGH (East London Genes and Health), COSMIC (Catalogue of Somatic Mutations in Cancers) and Gnomad (Genome Aggregation Database). The first version was published as part of Asifa et al., 2021, DOI to be included.
